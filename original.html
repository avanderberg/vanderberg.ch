Software Quality Assurance

I find the errors before your clients do—as a quality tester, I’m available on demand, for short projects, and qualified to test functionality, layout and security—giving your company the feeling of credibility, reliability and excellence it deserves.

With 15+ years experience in software quality assurance, I am well-equipped to steer your website, app or API to a place where it shines—without clunky errors, both obvious and harder to detect ones. I put your product through a series of sequential, thorough tests to be sure it is perfectly polished before it reaches the screen of your client.

Software quality assurance is the one step of product creation you’d be insane to skip—imagine how you feel when you click a broken link.
